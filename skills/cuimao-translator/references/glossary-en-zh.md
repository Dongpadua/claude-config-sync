## Translation Convention Notes

- **English names**: Keep as-is (John Smith, not 约翰·史密斯), unless a standard translation is universally recognized
- **Company/brand names**: Keep as-is (Microsoft, Apple, Google)
- **Book/article titles**: On first use, 中文译名（English Original Title）；subsequent uses can use just the Chinese
- **Academic citations**: Keep as-is "(Author, Year, p. N)"
- **Measurements**: Provide metric conversion in brackets if helpful: "10 miles (约16公里)"
